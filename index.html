Welcome to MOSAiC!

An interactive, browser-based tool to analyze and browse your mass spectrometry data.

Modern interface

Interact with your MS data!

Fast plotting

Exportable EICs for datasets with dozens of samples and thousands of molecular features

Molecular Networking

Load your GNPS networking results and compare related MS2 spectra

Previous Next

Find LC/MS features that are different between your samples, perform statistical analyses and create high-quality graphics for publication!

 

MOSAiC is currently under development, and new features are constantly added.

View on Github

 

Highlights:

- Run XCMS analyses locally on your computer without writing a script

- Find metabolites of interest in an MS feature table (generated with XCMS or other tools)

- Browse and plot extracted ion chromatograms of many samples and LC/MS features in parallel

- View mass spectra, predict molecular formulas

 

Coming soon:

- Search MSn databases

- Annotate Peaks

 

 

To stay on top of things, sign up for our newsletter to hear about new  developments!

 

MOSAiC is available as a web service, and as an R package.

 

You can try the tool in its current state, but please be aware that this is a preview version with restricted features.
